Configure DroidXMRMiner

Connect your wallet with the mining pool

info

Why SupportXMR?

We use SupportXMR as an example for its ease of use. You're free to explore and choose the pool you prefer ‚Äî the app includes several predefined pools and also allows custom configuration.

In DroidXMRMiner, go to Settings:

  1. 1

    In the Pool selector, choose pool.supportxmr.com:443

    TLS/SSL is automatically enabled when selecting port 443

  2. 2

    Paste your wallet address (95 characters) in the corresponding field

  3. 3

    Press Save to confirm the configuration

play_circle

Ready to mine!

Go back to the main screen and press Start Mining. The app will connect to the pool and you'll see the first stats in a few seconds.
